# Privacy Policy Draft For The Vehicle Lab

Effective date: July 6, 2026

The Vehicle Lab is a vehicle collection, customization, and racing app.

## Information We Collect

The current app does not collect personal information or send player data to a
server. Player names, vehicle collections, upgrades, race history, achievements,
and settings are stored locally on the device.

## Device Permissions

The current Android app does not request camera, microphone, location, contacts,
calendar, SMS, phone, Bluetooth, or network permissions.

## Local Save Data

The app may store game progress locally so players can keep their vehicle
collections and achievements. Users can remove local app data by deleting player
profiles in the app or clearing app storage in Android settings.

## Data Sharing

The current app does not share player data with third parties.

## Children

The Vehicle Lab is designed as a kid-friendly family app. The current app does
not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

If the app later adds online accounts, cloud backup, analytics, ads, purchases,
or other connected services, this policy should be updated before release.
