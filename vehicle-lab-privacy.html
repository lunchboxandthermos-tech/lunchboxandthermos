The Vehicle Lab Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Overview

The Vehicle Lab is developed by Lunchbox & Thermos Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how The Vehicle Lab handles information.

The Vehicle Lab is designed as a family-friendly vehicle collecting game. The app does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not sell user data.

Information We Collect

The Vehicle Lab does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, photos, contacts, payment information, or advertising identifiers.

Local App Data

The Vehicle Lab stores gameplay information locally on your device so the game can function properly. This may include:

  • Player profiles created inside the app
  • Vehicle collections
  • Inventory and Wish List progress
  • Race records
  • Achievements
  • Game settings and saved progress

This information stays on your device unless you choose to export, share, delete, or remove it.

Save Export and Import

The Vehicle Lab may allow users to export or import saved game data. Exported save files are created only when the user chooses to do so. Users are responsible for where they store or share exported files.

Children's Privacy

The Vehicle Lab may be used by children with adult supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Because the app does not collect personal information, no personal information from children is transmitted to Lunchbox & Thermos Studios.

Analytics

The Vehicle Lab does not currently use analytics tools to track user behavior.

Advertising

The Vehicle Lab does not display third-party advertisements.

Third-Party Services

The Vehicle Lab does not require third-party sign-in services, social media accounts, or external payment services.

Data Deletion

Because gameplay data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Lunchbox & Thermos Studios.

Email: lunchboxandthermos@gmail.com
