Privacy Policy

Sunny Stand by Lunchbox & Thermos Studios

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

Overview

Sunny Stand is designed as a local-first app for families. The app helps users run a small stand by tracking menu items, sales, inventory, goals, costs, payment preferences, and profit buckets.

Information We Collect

Sunny Stand does not collect, sell, or transmit personal information to Lunchbox & Thermos Studios.

The app does not require user accounts and does not ask for names, email addresses, phone numbers, birthdays, or location data.

Local Data Storage

Sunny Stand stores app data locally on your device so the app can function. This may include:

  • Menu items and prices
  • Sales history
  • Inventory and stock settings
  • Business goals
  • Startup costs
  • Profit allocation settings
  • Payment preference labels or handles entered by the user

This data remains on your device unless you delete the app, clear app data, or reset information within the app.

Children’s Privacy

Sunny Stand may be used by children with adult supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Payments

Sunny Stand may allow users to display payment information or QR codes for third-party payment services such as Venmo, Cash App, PayPal, or Zelle. These services are not operated by Lunchbox & Thermos Studios and are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Children should use payment features only with a grown-up.

Analytics

Sunny Stand does not collect analytics or usage data.

Advertising

Sunny Stand does not display third-party advertisements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data because we do not collect user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact
